About Us

Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar is a new sushi and Asian fusion restaurant opening in suburban Long Island, offering premium Japanese sushi alongside authentic Vietnamese and Thai dishes. Named after Hokkaido's historical identity, the restaurant aims to bring sophisticated, well-executed Asian cuisine to a community with limited dining options beyond Chinese takeout and hibachi chains. Positioned at mid-to-upscale pricing, Ezo welcomes diverse diners—from families to date-night couples—in a contemporary space that thoughtfully honors the distinct cultural traditions of Japanese, Vietnamese, and Thai culinary arts.

About The Owner

Ezo Kitchen & Sushi Bar is co-owned by Michael Chen and Daniel Martinez, who met at culinary institute and share a deep passion for Asian cuisine. Michael, a third-generation Japanese-American, trained under master sushi chefs in Los Angeles and Tokyo before transitioning to restaurant management. Daniel brings five years of immersive study across Asia, including formal kaiseki training in Kyoto and professional kitchen experience in Vietnam and Thailand.
